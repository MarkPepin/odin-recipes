World's Greatest Beef Jerky

Jack Links Ain't Got Nothing On Me

That'll be $15 sir. For what? A bag of air and some "beef" with enough chemicals to keep it "fresh" for a century? I think not.

Enter the homemade beef jerky. So lets fire up that dehydrator and get to work!

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs of Round Eye Beef
  • 3/4 cup of Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1/4 cup Rice Cooking Wine
  • 1/4 cup Brown Sugar
  • 2-3 TBSP Mirin
  • 2-3 TBSP Red Wine Vinegar
  • 2-3 TBSP Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 2-3 TBSP Rice Vinegar
  • 2-3 TSP Smoked Paprika
  • 2-3 TSP Liquid Smoke
  • 2-3 TSP Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1-3 TSP Ground Red Pepper/Cayenne
  • 1 in Ginger (cut into 4 chunks and crushed)
  • 5-7 Cloves Garlic (crushed to release oils)

Steps

  1. Slice beef against the grain
  2. Pound down to even thickness
  3. Prep marinade
  4. Marinade overnight in ziploc bag
  5. Dehydrate @ 160 for 4 hours
